# Privacy Policy — Clarity

**Last updated:** 2026-07-10

Clarity ("we", "us", "our") is operated by Haoze Li, an individual sole trader based in Australia.
Clarity is a maths tutoring platform intended for use by tutors and students located in Australia.
This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information in connection with
the Clarity application, in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the
*Privacy Act 1988* (Cth).

## 1. Who this policy covers

This policy applies to anyone who creates a Clarity account, as a tutor or a student, and to
information collected about students who are added to a tutor's roster.

## 2. Information we collect

- **Account information**: name, email address, and account role (tutor or student), collected at
  sign-up via Firebase Authentication (email/password or Google sign-in).
- **Roster relationships**: the tutor-student pairings a tutor creates on their roster.
- **Class information**: class titles, notes, schedules, attendee lists, and any files a tutor attaches
  as class materials.
- **Task information**: task titles, notes, due dates, student submissions, and any files attached to a
  task by either a tutor or a student.
- **Q&A messages**: the content of questions and answers exchanged between a tutor and a student
  through the app's Q&A feature.
- **Zoom connection information** (tutors only, optional): if a tutor chooses to connect their Zoom
  account, we store the Zoom account email associated with that connection and an encrypted copy of
  the OAuth access/refresh tokens Zoom issues, so we can create a Zoom meeting on the tutor's behalf
  when they schedule a class. We do not access anything else in a tutor's Zoom account beyond what is
  needed to create these meetings.
- **Technical/log data**: standard infrastructure logs generated by Google Cloud/Firebase as part of
  operating the service (e.g., request logs for the Cloud Functions backend), not directly reviewed by
  us except when investigating a fault or a reported security issue.

We do not collect payment information, government identifiers, or health information.

## 3. How we use this information

We use personal information only to operate Clarity's core features: creating and managing accounts,
scheduling and displaying classes, assigning and tracking tasks, enabling Q&A between tutors and
students, and (where a tutor opts in) creating Zoom meetings for scheduled classes. We do not use
personal information for advertising, and we do not sell personal information to any third party.

## 4. Who we share information with

- **Google (Firebase / Google Cloud)**: our infrastructure provider. All account data, class/task
  content, and file attachments are stored using Firebase Authentication, Firestore, and Firebase
  Storage, and our backend logic runs on Google Cloud Functions. Google acts as our service provider and
  does not use this data for its own purposes.
- **Zoom**: only for tutors who choose to connect their Zoom account, and only to the extent needed to
  create a meeting for a scheduled class (meeting topic, scheduled time, and duration are sent to Zoom's
  API). Zoom's own privacy policy governs Zoom's handling of that meeting data once created.
- We do not share personal information with any other third party, and we do not disclose it for
  marketing purposes.

## 5. Overseas storage and processing

Clarity is built on Google Cloud/Firebase infrastructure. While the service is intended for Australian
users, the underlying infrastructure may store and process personal information outside Australia,
including in the United States (for example, the backend functions that handle Zoom meeting creation
currently run in a United States Google Cloud region). By using Clarity, you acknowledge that your
information may be processed overseas as described here. We choose Google Cloud/Firebase specifically
because of its strong security practices (see Security Policy), and we take reasonable steps to ensure
information is protected consistently with the Australian Privacy Principles regardless of where it is
processed.

## 6. Data security

Personal information is protected using access controls enforced at the database level (Firestore and
Storage security rules), so that a user can only access data related to their own tutor-student
relationships. Zoom OAuth tokens receive an additional layer of encryption before being stored. See our
Security Policy (`security/policies/SECURITY_POLICY.md`) for further detail.

## 7. Data retention and deletion

We retain personal information for as long as an account remains active, so the app can continue to
function (e.g., displaying class history). If you would like your account or personal information
deleted, contact us at the email below and we will action the request manually — Clarity does not yet
have a fully self-service account-deletion feature, and we are noting that honestly here rather than
overstating our current capability.

## 8. Access and correction

You can request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you at any time by
contacting us at the email below. We will respond within a reasonable time.

## 9. Children and students under 18

Clarity is intended to be used by students aged 13 or older, or by younger students with the
involvement and consent of a parent or guardian. Tutors are responsible for ensuring they have
appropriate consent from a parent or guardian before adding a student who is a minor to their roster.
Clarity does not currently perform automated age verification at sign-up; we rely on the person
registering the account (or the tutor adding a student) to act honestly and appropriately here.

## 10. Complaints

If you believe we have mishandled your personal information, you can contact us directly at the email
below. If you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the
Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at [www.oaic.gov.au](https://www.oaic.gov.au).

## 11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time as Clarity's features change. The "Last updated" date at
the top of this document will reflect the most recent revision.
